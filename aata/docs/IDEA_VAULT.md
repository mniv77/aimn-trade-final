# IDEA VAULT

## Community Review Network

Every completed trade can be reviewed by the user.

The review form allows one or more evaluation tags.

Users can also enter free-form notes.

Reviews are stored in the local database for the user's own history.

If the user opts in,
anonymous review data is securely sent to the AATA servers.

The AI Professors study the combined experience of thousands of traders.

New lessons become new rules.

New rules improve future trading decisions.

---

## User Experience

Users feel appreciated because their opinions matter.

Instead of only receiving signals,
they help improve the Academy.

Every review becomes another lesson.

The community and the AI grow together.
