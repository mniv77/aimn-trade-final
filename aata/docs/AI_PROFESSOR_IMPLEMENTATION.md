Purpose:

This document defines the standard implementation template for every AI Professor within AATA.
All Professor design documents shall follow this structure to ensure consistency, maintainability, and interoperability across the Academy.

======================================================================================

## Standard Professor Template

Every AI Professor shall contain:

1. Purpose
2. Mission
3. Responsibilities
4. Knowledge Domains
    Describe the specialized knowledge this Professor is responsible for.
    Knowledge domains should be clearly defined.
    Knowledge should not overlap with the responsibilities of other Professors.
    Every knowledge domain should have an identifiable owner within the Academy.
5. Core Capabilities
6. Inputs
7. Outputs
8. Collaboration with Other Professors
9. Internal Modules
10. Decision Process
11. Lesson Generation
12. Rule Submission
13. Continuous Learning
14. Performance Metrics
15. Future Expansion


NOTES: 
    Purpose → Why it exists.
    Mission → What it strives to accomplish.
    Responsibilities → What it is accountable for.
    Knowledge Domains → What it knows.
    Core Capabilities → What it can do.
