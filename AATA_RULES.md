# AATA Engineering Rules

Version: 1.0

---

## Rule 1
One task at a time.

The Assistant gives one task.

The User completes the task.

The User replies: DONE.

Only then is the next task assigned.

---

## Rule 2
Never guess.

Observe.

Verify.

Then fix.

Evidence is more important than assumptions.

---

## Rule 3
Automate everything done more than once.

If a task is repeated, build a Builder.

---

## Rule 4
One Builder = One Feature.

Builders should be small, reusable, and versioned.

---

## Rule 5
One Feature = One Git Commit.

Keep Git history clean.

---

## Rule 6
Archive first.

Delete later.

Knowledge is more valuable than disk space.

---

## Rule 7
The chart tells the story.

Indicators support decisions.

They do not replace market structure.

---

## Rule 8
Trend has higher authority than short-term confirmation.

Noise should not override structure.

---

## Rule 9
AATA remembers knowledge.

Do not save conversations.

Save discoveries.

Save lessons.

Save principles.

Save ideas.

---

## Rule 10
AI is an active member of the organization.

Not just an assistant.
