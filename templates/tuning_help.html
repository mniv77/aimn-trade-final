What is Tuning?

Tuning is finding the "magic numbers" that make a strategy profitable for a specific asset. Each asset (BTC, ETH, AAPL, etc.) has unique behavior and requires its own optimized parameters.

You use TradingView to backtest hundreds of parameter combinations, then copy the winning settings into AiMN to trade live.

1 Open TradingView Strategy

1. Open your chart in TradingView (BTC/USD, ETH/USD, etc.)

2. Add the AiMN-G Tuning V31 strategy indicator

3. Select direction: BUY (for LONG) or SELL (for SHORT)

4. Open the Strategy Tester tab at the bottom

2 Find Magic Numbers (The Hard Part)

Your Goal: Maximize Total Profit

Parameters to Adjust:

  • RSI Length - Usually 4320 (3 days of 1-min bars) or 36 (3 days of 2-hour bars)
  • RSI Entry - For LONG: try 20-50. For SHORT: try 50-80
  • RSI Exit - For LONG: try 70-90. For SHORT: try 10-30
  • MACD Fast/Slow/Signal - Try unusual numbers! (5,24,9) or (22,14,8) might work
  • Stop Loss % - Usually 1-5%
  • Trailing Start/Drop - Advanced profit protection

IMPORTANT!

The MACD numbers don't need to "make sense" - they just need to produce profit! Fast=29, Slow=14, Signal=8 might seem backwards, but if it makes money, it works!

What You're Looking For:

  • Total Profit % as high as possible
  • Smooth equity curve (not choppy)
  • At least 20+ trades (proves it's not just luck)
  • Win rate above 40%

3 Copy the Orange Label (CSV String)

Once you find good numbers in TradingView:

1. Look for the orange label on your chart

2. It says 📋 V31 CSV (CLICK & COPY)

3. Click it and copy the entire string that appears

Example: BUY, 12, 26, 9, 4,320, 30, 0, 70, 0, 2, 0, 1, 0, 0.5, 0, 5, 0, ...

4 Sync to AiMN Dashboard

1. Go to the Tuning page

2. Select your Broker and Symbol

3. Select Direction (LONG or SHORT)

4. Paste the CSV string into the TradingView Bridge box

5. Click Map Indicators to Form

6. All fields auto-fill! ✅

7. Click SAVE SYSTEM

5 Understanding RSI Real (Range Scaling)

AiMN doesn't use standard 0-100 RSI. It uses Real-World Range Scaling.

Formula:

RSI Real = ((Current Price - MIN) / (MAX - MIN)) × 100

What this means:

  • MAX = Highest price in the last X bars (from RSI Length)
  • MIN = Lowest price in the last X bars
  • When price is near MAX → RSI Real is close to 100
  • When price is near MIN → RSI Real is close to 0

📊 Important: Copy MAX and MIN values!

Look at the HI/LO Copy Table in TradingView (bottom right)

Those numbers go in fields 22. RSI Real MAX and 23. RSI Real MIN

Pro Tips

✨ Don't Overtune

Numbers that work for 100+ trades are better than numbers that work perfectly for 10 trades.

🎯 Test Both Directions

BTC might be great for LONG but terrible for SHORT. Tune each direction separately.

📊 Different Timeframes

1h candles might work better than 2h for some assets. Try both!

🔄 Retune Regularly

Markets change. If profit drops, retune with fresh data.

Common Issues

⚠️ "RETUNE" warning on dashboard?
The price broke above MAX or below MIN. The range is outdated. Retune to get new MAX/MIN values.

❌ No matches (RSI or MACD stuck on WAIT)?
Your entry parameters might be too strict. Try widening the RSI Entry range or different MACD numbers.

🟡 Symbol shows "CLOSED" (yellow)?
Market is closed (Alpaca stocks only trade Mon-Fri 6:30am-1pm PST). Wait for market hours.
